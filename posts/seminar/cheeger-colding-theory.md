# About this Seminar

This seminar will discuss Cheeger-Colding theory, which studies the structure of Ricci limit spaces. Cheeger-Colding theory relies heavily on analysis tools, including classical integral inequalities and PDE estimates. Although analysis tools are important, the central point is to use these tools to study geometry, especially the structure of Ricci limit spaces.
